PhoneLockAI Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

PhoneLockAI is designed to help users reduce screen time and use their phones more intentionally.

Information We Collect

PhoneLockAI may collect and process:

  • Messages sent through the AI unlock chat
  • App settings and goals
  • Screen Time permission selections
  • Temporary unlock session information
  • Device identifiers used by third-party advertising services
  • Advertising data, such as rewarded ad impressions and ad interactions

How Information Is Used

We use this information to:

  • Provide app functionality
  • Process AI unlock requests
  • Manage unlock sessions and daily unlock limits
  • Show rewarded ads for additional unlocks
  • Improve app performance and reliability
  • Understand general usage patterns

Data Storage

Some data is stored locally on your device. Certain AI chat requests may be processed through secure backend services.

Third-Party Services

PhoneLockAI may use third-party services such as:

  • OpenAI APIs
  • Apple Screen Time APIs
  • Google AdMob

Google AdMob may collect device identifiers and advertising-related data to provide and measure ads. PhoneLockAI does not sell personal information to third parties.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information to third parties. Some data may be processed by third-party services only as needed to provide app functionality, AI responses, advertising, diagnostics, or reliability improvements.
